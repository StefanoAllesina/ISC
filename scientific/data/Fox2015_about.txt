Notes:
=====================================================================
MsID -> Manuscript ID. Each MsID is represented in multiple rows, one for each reviewer.
Year -> Submission Year
HandlingEditorSex -> Gender of handling editor
ReviewerSex -> Gender of reviewer
ReviewerRegion -> Geographic region of reviewer
ReviewerAgreed -> 1 for agree, 0 for decline
ReviewerScore -> Quality score, 1 being high and 4 being weak (see paper for details).
FinalDecision -> 1 Reject, 0 Accept
